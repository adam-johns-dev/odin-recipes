Wings

Description

Chicken wings fried with a blue cheese dip.

Ingredients

For Wings:

  • Chicken Wings (10)
  • 1tbps Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

For Dip:

  • Mayo
  • Sour Cream
  • Fresh Lemon Juice
  • Blue Cheese Crumbles

Method:

  1. Fry chicken wings for 25 minutes in air fryer
  2. Stir incredients for dip together
  3. Serve
